RF Communication Demo

May 15, 2022

Today I was working on another technology demonstration, one-way RF communications using the NRF24L01 modules, interfacing with Arduino. The devices communicate on the 2.5 GHz ISM band, with pressing the button on the device on the left transmitting a btye, and the device on the right listening for a specific byte on the specified frequency. The NRF24L01 modules transmit using gaussian frequency shift keying, or GSFK. In the future I plan on implementing a bidirectional telemetry link for transmitting sensor data and sending and receiving commands for my next generation of rocket avionics.



3D Printed Rocket Components

May 9, 2022

I'm currently experimenting with 3D printing structural components for my rocketry projects. I've made an almost 1:1 copy of a commercial 33mm conical nosecone. The commercial styrene part is still 55% lighter than my 3D Printed PLA part, however playing with infill and wall thickness could probably lower this number substantially. I'm really pleased with the print quality. I plan on 3D Printing most if not all of the structural parts for my future rocketry projects.

Design Overhaul

May 2, 2022

I've completely overhauled the visual design and UI of my website. After hours of painstaking prototyping and shuffling through decades-old stackexchange posts, it finally works properly. Using the CSS flex property, and some clever use of classes, I was able to make a sticky footer that properly scales with the content of this site. In addition, I've learned to use the CSS @media query to scale the header of this site for mobile devices. Lastly, I added a cool background image to complement this site's accent colors. It's one of my custom circuit board designs! In the extremely near future I plan on adding an "archive" section to this site, where I can put all of the projects I've built throughout high school before this site was launched.
